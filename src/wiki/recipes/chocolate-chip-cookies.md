## ingredients

- 170 g (less than 1 cup) granulated sugar
- 125 g butter (equivalent to 100 ml melted)
- 1 egg
- 210 g (2 cups) flour
- 1 level tbsp. cornstarch
- 2 tbsp. molasses or 1 tbsp. extract
- ½ tsp. salt
- ½ tsp. baking soda
- 1 packet vanilla sugar
- 120 g finely chopped chocolate

## instructions

1. **melt the butter** and pour it into your mixing bowl
2. add the **sugar** to the melted butter and mix by hand for 1–2 minutes until combined
3. add the **egg** and continue mixing for another 1–2 minutes
4. stir in the **molasses**, **baking soda**, and **salt**
5. once well combined, add the **flour**, **cornstarch** and **vanilla sugar**. mix until a dough forms
6. fold in the **chopped chocolate**
7. **chill the dough** in the refrigerator for at least **30 minutes**
8. pre-heat your oven to **170°C (340°F)** (upper-lower heat mode)
9. Roll pieces of dough (slightly larger than a walnut) and place them on a baking tray
10. **bake for 13–14 minutes**. The edges should look baked, but the center should still be soft and sink slightly when pressed
11. let them cool on the tray before serving so they can set
