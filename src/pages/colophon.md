### stack

- runtime: [Deno](https://deno.com/)
- static site generator [Lume](https://lume.deno.dev/)
- CSS framework [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/)
- fonts: Josefin Sans & Inconsolata
- icons: mostly [Lucide](https://lucide.dev/), sometimes [Simple Icons](https://simpleicons.org/)

### credits

- Lightbox & Media: The media grid and high-density timeline are heavily inspired by and adapted from [Phanpy](https://phanpy.social/), an incredible Mastodon client by [cheeaun](https://cheeaun.com/). Licensed under MIT.
